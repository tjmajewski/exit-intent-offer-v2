# Pop-up QA: Make Sure Every Pop-up Fits Your Store

**Who it's for:** All plans
**Where to find it:** Resparq sidebar → Pop-up QA
**Time to complete:** About 5 minutes

---

## What this is

Resparq can show your visitors eight different pop-up styles. Most stores look
great with all eight. But a style that fits one theme can clash with another:
a bar across the top might overlap your announcement bar, or a bottom drawer
might cover a sticky cart.

Pop-up QA lets you check each style on your real store and turn off any that
don't fit. The ones you keep on are the only styles your shoppers will ever see.

---

## The eight layouts at a glance

Each card on the Pop-up QA page shows a small wireframe so you can see where the
pop-up sits before you preview it:

| Layout | Where it appears |
|---|---|
| Classic Card | Centered box over a dimmed page |
| Top Banner | Slim bar across the top |
| Bottom Sheet | Drawer anchored to the bottom |
| Coupon Ticket | Centered ticket with a dashed edge |
| Split Hero | Two-panel box, image beside the offer |
| Timer Front | Centered box with a live countdown |
| Testimonial | Centered box with a star rating |
| Scratch Reveal | Scratch-off card that reveals the offer |

The wireframe is a quick orientation. The button below it shows you the real
thing on your live theme.

## How to QA your pop-ups

### 1. Open Pop-up QA

In the Resparq sidebar, click **Pop-up QA**. You'll see all eight layouts, each
marked **On** or **Off**.

### 2. Turn on the app embed (one time)

Preview shows the pop-up on your actual storefront, which needs the Resparq app
embed switched on:

> Online Store → Themes → Customize → App embeds → enable Resparq

If you've already finished Resparq setup, this is already on.

### 3. Preview a layout

Click **Preview on store** on any card. Your storefront opens in a new tab with
that pop-up showing, using sample text. Look for anything that clashes:

- Does it overlap your header, announcement bar, or sticky cart?
- Do the colors fight with your theme?
- On mobile, does it cover something important?

Previewing is completely safe. Nothing is tracked, no discount is created, and
your shoppers don't see it. It's just for you.

### 4. Turn off anything that doesn't fit

Back on the Pop-up QA tab, click **Disable** on any layout that clashed. It
flips to **Off** and Resparq stops showing it to shoppers. Changed your mind?
Click **Enable** to turn it back on.

You can always keep it simple: if you're happy with all eight, you don't have to
do anything.

---

## Good to know

- **You can't turn off every layout.** At least one has to stay on so your
  shoppers always have something to see. If you try to disable the last one,
  Resparq will stop you.
- **Turning a layout off never deletes anything.** It's fully reversible. Turn
  it back on any time.
- **Just disabled a popular style?** You might see a note that some recent
  pop-ups are temporarily showing as Classic Card. That's expected. Resparq
  swaps anything you turned off for a safe style, and it clears itself up as the
  AI refreshes its lineup.
- **Manual mode:** If you run Resparq in Manual mode, your store always uses the
  single layout you chose in Settings, so these on/off switches don't change
  what shoppers see. They take effect when you switch to AI mode and let Resparq
  pick layouts for you. Preview still works in either mode.

## Quick troubleshooting

**Preview opens but no pop-up appears.**
The app embed is probably off. Turn it on under Online Store → Themes →
Customize → App embeds, then preview again.

**The Enable/Disable button didn't seem to save.**
You'll see a short message at the bottom of the screen confirming each change.
If you see "Try again" instead, your layouts were left as they were. Refresh and
try once more.

**I want all eight back the way they started.**
Click **Enable** on every layout marked Off. That returns you to the default,
where Resparq can use all eight.
